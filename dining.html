24-Hour Room Service

Guests can enjoy freshly prepared meals and beverages from the comfort of their rooms at any time of the day. Our room service menu includes breakfast favourites, gourmet sandwiches, salads, pizzas, pasta, grilled dishes, desserts, and beverages, delivered promptly by our dedicated hospitality team.

Signature Chef's Specialties

Our Executive Chef and culinary team take pride in creating signature dishes that showcase fresh seasonal ingredients and exceptional craftsmanship. Guests can enjoy premium grilled steaks, Atlantic salmon, roasted herb chicken, handmade pasta, gourmet burgers, fresh seafood, locally inspired dishes, and an impressive selection of handcrafted desserts. Every plate is carefully prepared to deliver a memorable dining experience.

Dietary Options

We are pleased to accommodate a variety of dietary preferences and requirements. Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and low-sodium meal options are available upon request. If you have food allergies or special dietary needs, please inform our restaurant team, who will be happy to assist you.

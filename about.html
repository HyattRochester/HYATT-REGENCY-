Welcome to Hyatt Regency Hotel Rochester

Located in the heart of downtown Rochester, New York, Hyatt Regency Hotel Rochester welcomes guests with a perfect blend of contemporary comfort, exceptional hospitality, and timeless elegance. Whether you are visiting for business, a family vacation, a romantic getaway, or a special celebration, our hotel is thoughtfully designed to provide a memorable stay from the moment you arrive. For years, Hyatt Regency Rochester has been recognised as a preferred destination for travellers seeking premium accommodation, personalised service, and convenient access to the city's most popular attractions. Our experienced team is dedicated to ensuring every guest enjoys a warm welcome, attentive care, and an atmosphere that reflects the highest standards of hospitality. The hotel features beautifully designed guest rooms and spacious suites, offering modern furnishings, luxurious bedding, high-speed internet access, smart televisions, workspaces, and breathtaking views of the Rochester skyline. Every detail has been carefully considered to create an environment where guests can relax, recharge, and feel completely at home. Guests can enjoy outstanding dining experiences prepared by talented chefs who combine fresh local ingredients with international flavours to create memorable meals throughout the day. From delicious breakfasts and elegant dinners to handcrafted beverages and room service, every meal is prepared with quality and care. Hyatt Regency Rochester also offers versatile meeting rooms, conference facilities, and elegant event spaces suitable for corporate meetings, weddings, receptions, training sessions, and social gatherings. Supported by professional event planners and modern technology, every event is delivered with excellence and attention to detail. Our commitment extends beyond comfortable accommodation. We strive to create lasting relationships with our guests by providing outstanding customer service, maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness, embracing innovation, and continuously improving every aspect of the guest experience. Whether you are staying for one night or an extended visit, Hyatt Regency Rochester remains dedicated to delivering exceptional hospitality, sophisticated comfort, and unforgettable experiences that inspire guests to return again and again.
